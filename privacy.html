Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 25, 2025

Welcome to MIDI Drum Remapper. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we treat your personal information and your privacy rights.

1. What Data We Collect

1.1 MIDI Files

When you upload MIDI files to our service:

  • The MIDI files are processed entirely in your browser
  • Files are not stored on our servers
  • We do not have access to the content of your MIDI files
  • All processing happens locally on your device

1.2 Mapping Presets

When you save custom mapping presets:

  • Presets are stored in your browser's local storage
  • They remain on your local device only
  • We do not collect or store your custom mappings on our servers

1.3 Usage Data

We collect anonymous usage data through standard web analytics to understand how users interact with our service, including:

  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on the site
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referral source

This data is anonymized and cannot be used to personally identify you.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the limited data we collect to:

  • Improve our website functionality and user experience
  • Analyze usage patterns to optimize our service
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Develop new features based on how users interact with the tool

3. Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies to remember your preferences, such as:

  • Dark/light mode preferences
  • UI settings to enhance your experience

We also use analytics cookies to understand how visitors use our website. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics for anonymous usage statistics
  • CDN services to serve static content quickly

These services may collect anonymous data subject to their own privacy policies.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of analytics tracking
  • Clear local storage data through your browser settings

Since we process MIDI files entirely in your browser and don't store them on our servers, you maintain full control over your musical content.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

We recommend that you review this privacy policy periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:

support@onlinemidiremap.com
